I am interested in knots, links, and surfaces in 4-manifolds. In particular, I am interested in questions related to sliceness, minimality of knot cobordisms in general, and how they relate to knot homology theories.



### Squeezed knots and satellite operations (in preparation)
We give a classification result for cables of positive braid knots and a proof that the cable of any squeezed knot is squeezed. We also show that given any choice of companion knot K and any integer m, the (m,n)-cable of K is squeezed provided n is sufficiently large (or small). This gives an explanation to an observation of Van Cott.
