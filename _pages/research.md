### Squeezed knots and satellite operations (in preparation)
Feller, Lewark, and Lobb recently introduced the notion of a "squeezed knot": a knot that arises as a slice of a genus-minimizing connected, oriented cobordism between a positive torus knot and a negative torus knot.
We give a classification result for cables of positive (and negative) braid knots and leverage this into a proof that the cable of any squeezed knot is squeezed.
