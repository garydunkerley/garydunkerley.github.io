I am a first year graduate student at the University of Georgia where I intend to study topology. 

After earning my B.Sc. in mathematics and literature at Hillsdale College, I spent a year in Providence, RI bumming classes at Brown. At the encouragment of [Jeffrey Hoffstein](https://www.brown.edu/academics/math/people/jeffrey-hoffstein), I earned my masters degree at Brandeis University where I studied knot invariants under [Daniel Ruberman](https://people.brandeis.edu/~ruberman/).

When not doing math, I enjoy gardening, cooking, and hanging out with Kimchi (my cat).
