The Jones polynomial is a knot invariant capable of distinguishing the handedness of chiral knots. It can be defined by performing certain manipulations on knot diagrams, but there is also a three-dimensional description discovered by Witten in Chern-Simmons theory where it arises as a consequence of particle motion through a gauge field.
