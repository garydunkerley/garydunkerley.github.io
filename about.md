My interest in math began after reading a [particular essay](https://www.maa.org/sites/default/files/pdf/devlin/LockhartsLament.pdf) while attending Hillsdale College. I later enrolled in the masters program at Brandeis and learned about a bunch of beautiful math. I am interested in investigating questions in the field of [low dimensional topology](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Low-dimensional_topology), some of which emerged from a cross-pollination with [ideas from physics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gauge_theory). I am also curious about [information geometry](https://faculty.coe.drexel.edu/jwalsh/Yunshu_InformationGeometry.pdf).

I spend my spare time cooking fun vegetarian dishes, playing go, or hanging out with [my cat](https://i.imgur.com/92WV3Ga.jpg). 

My [sister](https://caradunkerley.com/) is an MFA candidate at the Art Institute of Chicago.
