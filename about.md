My interest in math began after reading a [particular essay](https://www.maa.org/sites/default/files/pdf/devlin/LockhartsLament.pdf) while attending Hillsdale College. I later enrolled in the masters program at Brandeis and learned even more beautiful math. I am interested in investigating questions related in low dimensional topology, some of which emerged from a cross-pollination with ideas from physics. 

I have lots of other interests, but the pandemic has largely restricted me to making my music collection larger, playing go, and hanging out with [my best friend](https://i.imgur.com/92WV3Ga.jpg). My [sister](https://caradunkerley.com/) is an MFA candidate at the Art Institute of Chicago.
